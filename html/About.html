Why we chose this theme

Cape Town is one of the most photographed cities in the world, but almost all of that attention lands on the same handful of landmarks - Table Mountain, the V&A Waterfront, Camps Bay. We wanted to catalog the things locals walk past every day without a second thought: a cannon fired by hand at noon, a memorial built from bones uncovered during construction, a coffee shop disguised as a Victorian workshop. Places with a real story, but no queue of tourists.
